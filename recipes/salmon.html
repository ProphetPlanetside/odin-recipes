Salmon Sushi

A classic and simple Japanese dish best made with fresh Salmon and Sushi Rice.

This recipe contains the steps you need to create your very own Salmon Nigiri Sushi!

Ingredients

  • Unseasoned Rice Vinegar
  • Granulated Sugar
  • Salt
  • Short-grain Rice
  • Sushi-grade Salmon
  • Wasabi
  • Soy Sauce

Steps

  1. Stir vinegar, sugar, and salt in a bowl until dissolved.
  2. Rinse rice with water; cook.
  3. Spread rice in a large glass baking dish; drizzle with vinegar mixture.
  4. Form rice mixture into 16 3x1 inch pieces.
  5. Top the rice pieces with Salmon slices.
  6. Serve with Wasabi and Soy Sauce.
