Orange Chicken

A classic Chinese dish combining chicken with a delicious orange sauce.

This recipe contains the steps you need to create your very own Orange Chicken!

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast
  • Salt
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Cornstarch
  • Orange Zest, Orange Juice
  • Brown Sugar
  • Garlic
  • Seasoned rice wine vinegar
  • Steamed Rice

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375F.
  2. In a large bowl, toss the chicken with salt and vegetable oil. Place chicken pieces on a baking sheet.
  3. Bake chicken for about 20 minutes (until cooked through).
  4. In a small bowl, prepare orange sauce by combining the cornstarch, orange zest, orange juice, soy sauce, garlic, sugar, rice wine vinegar, and water.
  5. Bring sauce to simmer over medium-high heat. Simmer until thickened. Remove from heat.
  6. Pour sauce over chicken and toss chicken until fully coated.
  7. Serve over white rice. Sprinkle sesame seeds and green onions if desired.
