Lasagna

A classic Italian dish of big noodles, sauce, and beef.

This recipe contains the steps you need to create your very own Lasagna!

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Mozarella, Ricotta, and Parmesan Cheeses
  • Lean Ground Beef
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Pasta Sauce
  • Fresh Parsley
  • Egg
  • Italian Seasoning

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 350F. Cook pasta al dente.
  2. Cook Beef until brown.
  3. Stir Pasta Sauce and Italian Seasoning; simmer.
  4. Combine the 3 cheeses and add the egg.
  5. Put lasagna noodles with sauce, meat, and cheese in the pan.
  6. Cover with foil and bake for 45 minutes.
  7. Take Lasagna out of the oven and sprinkle cheese on the top.
  8. Let rest for 10 minutes before cutting.
